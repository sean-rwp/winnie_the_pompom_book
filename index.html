Meet Winnie

Winnie may be tiny, and her health may throw her curveballs — but she never lets that slow her down. Always positive, always trying her best, she proves that heart matters more than size in this rhyming, read-aloud adventure through her sunny Los Angeles world.

Every page was lovingly illustrated by Ben Girdwood from real photos of the real Winnie — no AI. This self-published board book was made for little readers ages 0–5, and for anyone who needs a reminder that big spirit comes in small packages.
